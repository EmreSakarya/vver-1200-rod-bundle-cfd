Star-CCM+ heat-generation field functions
==========================================
(VVER-1200 19-rod hexagonal mini-bundle, sinusoidal axial power source)

Outer rods  (r > 18 mm, power factor f = 0.97):
  5.6346e8 * sin(3.14159265359 * $$Position[2] / 3.75)

Inner rods  (r <= 18 mm, power factor f = 0.88):
  5.1118e8 * sin(3.14159265359 * $$Position[2] / 3.75)

Radial selector (true => outer-ring rod):
  sqrt( ($$Position[0]*$$Position[0]) + ($$Position[1]*$$Position[1]) ) > 0.018

Central guide tube: q''' = 0 (no heat source assigned).

Units: W/m^3 ; $$Position[2] is the axial coordinate z in metres, z in [0, 0.375].
Core-average volumetric heat rate q_bar = 3.698e8 W/m^3 over the full 3.75 m height;
the 0.5*pi normalization in the prefactors restores the core-average once the
sin profile is integrated.
